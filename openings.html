Join Us!

We are actively looking for new people to join our team studying the genetics of chromatin and gene regulation. Our current focus is to use human genetic variation to understand how chromatin state and organization are specified within immune cells. We are also interested in linking genetic variation in the immune system to autoimmune and infectious disease risk.

In our research we perform sophisticated statistical and computational analysis of data from genomics experiments such as ChIP-seq, ATAC-seq and Hi-C. We welcome applications from individuals with diverse experimental and computational backgrounds in areas such as genetics, computer science, statisics, and molecular biology.
